## About

I'm an AI researcher working at the intersection of **mechanistic interpretability**, **retrieval-augmented generation**, and **multi-agent reasoning systems**. My work asks mechanistic questions — not just _what_ language models do, but _why_ they do it, and how we can build architectures that produce more reliable, trustworthy behaviour.

Currently at **Lexsi Labs** investigating whether machine unlearning produces genuine circuit disruption or merely suppresses behavioural expression of internal knowledge states. My MS thesis at IISER Bhopal developed a dialectical engine (DARE) that resolves epistemic conflicts in RAG systems through formal cross-examination — achieving 77% improvement on FaithEval and 28% on RAMDocs.
